What is SkyPort?

In short, SkyPort is a hub for sharing your experiences stargazing.


Whether you want to share the awe of the Moon you spent hours watching last night or take a glimpse into others' stargazing adventures, SkyPort allows users to interact with astronomers from near and far.


Here, you can post about your own sightings, comment on others' sightings, or browse the feed of some of the most beautiful objects in the universe.
